Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

The Journey Begins

Founded in 2010 by a group of automotive enthusiasts with a shared vision, SHIFT & DRIFT started as a small local dealership with just five vehicles and a passion for exceptional customer service.

Over the years, our unwavering commitment to quality, transparency, and automotive excellence has enabled us to grow into one of the nation's most trusted names in the industry, serving thousands of satisfied customers annually.

Today, our state-of-the-art facilities span across multiple locations, featuring advanced service centers, comfortable showrooms, and a handpicked inventory of premium vehicles that meet our stringent quality standards.

Our Commitments

Making a difference beyond business

Community Involvement

Through our "Wheels for Change" initiative, we donate a portion of every sale to local transportation programs for underprivileged families, ensuring everyone has access to reliable transportation for work, education, and healthcare.

We also organize annual community events, car shows, and educational workshops to share our automotive knowledge and bring car enthusiasts together.

Environmental Responsibility

As automotive technology evolves, so do we. We're proud to offer an expanding selection of electric and hybrid vehicles, along with resources to help our customers understand and transition to more sustainable driving options.

Our facilities implement energy-efficient practices, and we partner with local organizations for responsible recycling of automotive materials.
